Full Bootstrap Prompt

Hey Hermes, you just got installed on my machine. Help me set you up properly.

Quick heads up before we start: my answers in Part 1 become long-term context that gets sent to your upstream LLM provider on every future call. I will keep them useful but skip secrets, salary, health info, and anything under NDA.

We will do this in three parts. One question at a time, wait for my answer before moving on, never batch.

PART 1: GET TO KNOW ME
Ask me 6 short questions to build a profile:

1. Name and what I do (role, who I work with)
2. The biggest project on my plate right now
3. My top 3 goals for the next 6 months
4. Preferred communication style (terse / detailed / casual / professional)
5. Time zone and typical working hours
6. Daily tools I use that you should know about

After all 6 answers, draft a summary of what you learned and show it to me first. Wait for me to confirm or edit before writing anything to disk. Once I confirm, save to USER.md (or your equivalent) and show me the file path plus what was written.

PART 2: DAILY QUESTIONS
Each morning, ask me 3 questions you pick to help you get to know me better. You choose the questions yourself - they should vary day to day, build on what you already know, and dig into gaps you have not filled yet (interests, work rhythm, things I am stuck on, what I am proud of, what I am avoiding).

Save my answers to memory after each session so the next day's questions can build on them.

Ask me which platform (whichever messaging surface you are connected to: Telegram, Discord, Slack, Email, CLI) and what time. Before scheduling the cron, send me a test message NOW on that platform so we verify the connection works. If delivery fails, surface the error and do NOT write the cron. If it works, save the cron and show me the expression plus the file you wrote it to.

PART 3: 3 THINGS YOU CAN DO FOR ME
Based on what you know about me (Part 1 plus any Part 2 sessions), suggest 3 specific automations or proactive behaviours you can run for me. Pick things that fit my profile, not generic agent demos. For each, give me:

What you would do (one sentence, plain English)
How often (one-off / daily / weekly / monthly)
What you need from me to start (API key, folder access, account login)
Risk level (read-only / writes to my files / writes to external services / sends messages on my behalf) plus one sentence on what could go wrong

Wait for me to pick which ones I want. Set them up one at a time, confirming each is live before moving to the next.
